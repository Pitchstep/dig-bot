Privacy Policy for "DIG Stuff" Bot

Effective Date: July 2025

DIG Stuff Bot ("we", "our", or "the Bot") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data when you use our Discord bot.

1. Information We Collect

  • User Data: We collect your Discord user ID and username only when you interact with the bot (e.g., posting an LFG, clicking buttons).
  • Messages: The bot may access messages you send as part of commands or interactions.
  • Server Data: We access server ID, channel ID, roles, and permissions only to provide bot functionality.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide and improve the bot’s features.
  • To manage user interactions such as tracking who is interested in an LFG post.
  • To moderate and manage content (e.g., deleting LFG posts on request).

3. Data Sharing

We do NOT share your personal data with third parties except as required by law or Discord’s policies.

4. Data Retention

Data related to user interactions is retained only as long as needed to maintain functionality or fulfill user requests. We do NOT store data permanently outside of Discord’s infrastructure.

5. Your Rights

  • You may request information about what data we hold on you.
  • You may request deletion of your data by contacting the bot owner or server admins.

6. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data but cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. We encourage you to review it regularly.

Contact

For any questions or requests, please contact the bot owner or the server admins.
